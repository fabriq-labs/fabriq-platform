# Youtube Analytics

### Prerequisite

The report will be available within 48 hours of creating the reporting job and will be for the day that the job was scheduled.

### Configuration

1. Start by clicking **Connect** to initiate the OAuth process. Once the OAuth process is successfully completed, a select box will be automatically updated with the current value.

2. Next, log in and authorize your Instagram account.

3. Finally, click **Continue** to proceed with the configuration.

By following these steps and updating the necessary details in the Fabriq UI, you can successfully create the Youtube source connector for your account.
