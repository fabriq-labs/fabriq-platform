# Instagram

To create an Instagram source connector, please follow the instructions outlined below:

### Prerequisite

Ensure that your Instagram business account is connected to your Facebook page.

### Configuration

1. Start by clicking **Connect** to initiate the OAuth process. Once the OAuth process is successfully completed, a select box will be automatically updated with the current value.

2. Next, log in and authorize your Instagram account.

3. Select a start date to specify the beginning of your data collection.

4. Finally, click **Continue** to proceed with the configuration.

By following these steps and updating the necessary details in the Fabriq UI, you can successfully create the Instagram source connector for your account.
